Company history

The Limited Liability Company "NISHA" was established in 1991. The main and almost the only activity at that time, was the production, purchase and sale of technical casein. The market demand for this product has enabled the company to take a certain place in the domestic market and enter the external one.

A well-thought-out financial policy, reliable partners, high quality of products allowed us not to reduce the momentum gained in the difficult 90s, avoid the crisis in 1998 and continue the successful development of the company in the 2000s. During these years, LLC “NISHA” helped in the modernization of dairy plants

A line for the production of glazed curd bars was installed at the Osipovichi Dairy Plant in Osipovichi, Republic of Belarus. Lines for the production of technical casein were manufactured and implemented at Russian factories: “Mozhga-cheese", Mozhga, SMK “Rosa”, Smolensk, Kez cheese factory, Kez, as well as the Republic of Belarus: SVH “October", Grodno, Mogilev Dairy plant, Mogilev.

A line for the production of food grade casein was purchased and installed at the JSC “Lyakhovichi Dairy Plant” in Lyakhovichi. Along with this, LLC “NISHA” participated in the state program of the Republic of Belarus for the supply of combine harvesters manufactured in Germany to the Brest and Minsk regions.

Is this not evidence of high qualifications and professionalism?

Since the beginning of the 2000s, the production of food grade casein has been mastered at Joint JSC "Lyakhovichi Dairy Plant". LLC "NISHA" began to expand the range of products, giving priority to dairy.
