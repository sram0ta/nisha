Marble cheese with a mass fraction of fat in dry matter 45%

Smell and taste: Pronounced cheesy,spicy salty with a specific taste and smell as a result of the action of the enzyme lipase.
Consistency: Dense, slightly brittle, light crystallization is allowed.
Expiration date: 120 days at a storage temperature from 0 ° C to plus 4 ° C.

Manufacturers

  • JSC "Zdravushka - Milk" branch of the Slutsk cheese factory"

Description

Physical and chemical properties

Ingredients: Normalized pasteurized cow's milk, non–iodized table salt,
bacterial starter culture of lactic acid microorganisms, calcium chloride,
milk-converting enzyme preparation of microbial origin,food additives:
enzyme preparation lipase, enzyme preparation of animal origin - lysozyme.

Name of the indicator: The standard for the product
Mass fraction of fat in dry matter 45,0,0±1,6 %
Mass fraction of moisture No less than 41%
The mass fraction of moisture in the fat-free substance 49-56% inclusive
Mass fraction of table salt 0,5-2,5%
